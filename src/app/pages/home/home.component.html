Vision & Mission

To equip and inspire aspiring IAS officers across India with the knowledge, skills and confidence needed to succeed. We strive to develop a dynamic learning environment, shaping the future with ethical, dedicated leaders for the country.

Veranda IAS provides a vibrant environment for Civil Services Exam aspirants through rigorous preparation. We provide pre-planned class schedules every month with follow up test series for both prelims and mains examinations, peer group learning, guest lectures to make them a service-ready candidate.

“I am immensely pleased to be part of this unique and novel holistic residential programme. It is a first of its kind in the nation & I genuinely hope & truly believe that the students who are here will utilise the opportunity to the best of their advantage. I hope they set clear goals, plan meticulously, work hard, strain every nerve & crack the UPSC CSE.”
Sagayam IAS (VR)
Chief Mentor of Veranda IAS
“Today, it's possible for the daughter of a farmer in a remote village or a student studying in less renowned colleges in obscure towns to master the intricacies of preparing the subjects for UPSC exams. There are brilliant minds scattered throughout India, waiting to be nurtured, so that they can contribute meaningfully to our nation's governance and progress as a bureaucrat.”
Bharat Seeman
CEO of Veranda IAS
