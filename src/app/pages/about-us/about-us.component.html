About Veranda IAS

Crafting India's future leaders! One UPSC aspirant at a time!

Veranda IAS offers India's first holistic residential programme for students preparing for the UPSC Civil Services Examination. Our state-of-the-art facilities, comfortable accommodation, healthy meals and round-the-clock faculty support ensure a distraction-free verdant environment for students to immerse themselves in their studies. Our programme also includes extracurricular activities to promote wellness and community-building. Join us at Veranda IAS and achieve your goal of cracking the UPSC Civil Services Examination.
